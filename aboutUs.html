About Us

Background

Hailing from Pittsburgh Pa. Bridget Lee always had an "eye for design". "Ever since I was a little kid I always tried to put things together and designing little small things for my friends." Her small hobby turned into more around 2018 when she was tasked to do the planning for a wedding. The wedding had rave reviews and she realized she would like to expand her interests. She has been tasked to do everything from wedding planning to non-profit events. Although this company is new, it is rapidly expanding in the Richmond Va area where Bridget relocated around 10 years ago.
